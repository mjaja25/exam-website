Exam Instructions

General Rules

  • The exam consists of 3 stages: Typing, Letter, and Excel.
  • Each stage is timed for 5 minutes.
  • Once you start, you cannot pause the test.
  • Refreshing or closing the page is disabled during the test.

1. Typing Test (20 Marks)

You must achieve at least 35 WPM with 95% accuracy to qualify. Your final score is based on your speed and precision.

2. Letter Test (10 Marks)

You will be given a prompt to write a formal letter. An AI will grade your response on its format, content, and grammar.

3. Excel Test (20 Marks)

You will download an Excel file, complete the tasks inside, and re-upload it. An AI will grade your submission against the correct solution.
